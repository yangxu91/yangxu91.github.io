A short introduction

Xu Yang is currently an Associate Professor with the College of Computer and Data Science, Minjiang University, China. He received the Ph.D. degree from the School of Science, RMIT University, Australia, with Data61, CSIRO in 2021, and used to be a Postdoctoral Researcher with the School of Mathematics and Statistics, Fujian Normal University. His research interests include cryptography and information security.
